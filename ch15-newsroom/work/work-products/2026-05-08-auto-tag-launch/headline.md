## Headline candidates, ranked by voice fit

  1. Tuesday morning, the support inbox tags itself.
  2. Auto-tag goes live Friday — we taught it on a year of your
     team's messages.
  3. The first thing customer-support teams stop doing this week.
  4. We watched our beta customers stop sorting their inbox.
     This is what they replaced it with.
  5. Auto-tag, in production, finally.
  6. Why we built auto-tag (and what it doesn't try to do).
  7. The activation time you'll get back this Friday.
  8. Auto-tag is here. Your support inbox already knew.

Notes on ranking: #1 opens with a concrete moment (your-tone rule).
#2 is the safe choice — informative but less surprising. #3 is the
contrast play. #5 violates the anti-list ("finally" feels like
throat-clearing). #6 is meta and tonally wrong for a launch.
Recommend #1 or #3 — both feel most like Maya's voice. #1 has
better SEO (search-engine optimization — how easily Google finds
it); #3 has more emotional pull.
