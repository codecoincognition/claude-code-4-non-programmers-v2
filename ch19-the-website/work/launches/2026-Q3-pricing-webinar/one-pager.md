# Pricing for SaaS founders — one-pager (value bullets + objections)

## What you'll walk out with
- A pricing audit of your current tiers
- The four mistakes that show up in 80% of pricing pages
- A worksheet for repricing tier 2 by next Friday
- The recording, in case you can't make it live

## Objections (source for the FAQ)
- "Is this for early-stage or scale-stage?" — Both. Framework scales from
  "no pricing yet" to "five tiers and a fight."
- "Will the recording be available?" — Yes, within 24h, to everyone who
  registers.
- "Is this a thinly veiled course pitch?" — No. One mention at the end,
  never again.
- "Can I bring my co-founder?" — Yes. One seat per registration; two of you
  is two registrations, same email domain is fine.
