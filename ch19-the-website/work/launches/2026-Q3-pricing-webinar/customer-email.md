# Customer-launch email — voice + tone reference for the page copy

Subject: The pricing decision that's quietly costing you 30%

Hey {first_name},

Quick one. I'm running a free live workshop next Wednesday on the single
pricing decision most SaaS founders get wrong — your tier-2 plan.

No fluff, no 90-minute pitch. Sixty minutes, a live audit, real Q&A, and a
worksheet you can use the same week. Everyone who registers gets the
recording.

Save your seat → {landing_page_url}

— Maya

---
Voice notes (for the landing page):
- Plain, direct, a little dry. No exclamation marks.
- Short sentences. One idea per line.
- "Free, live, with Q&A" is the recurring promise — keep it verbatim.
- Never oversell. The honesty IS the pitch.
