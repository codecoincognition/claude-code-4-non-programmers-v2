# Vendor Agreement — AWS Compute Savings Plan

**Counterparty:** Amazon Web Services, Inc.
**Type:** Compute Savings Plan (commitment)
**Effective date:** 2025-02-20
**Term:** 12-month commitment
**Auto-renew:** No — the plan expires at term end and reverts to on-demand
pricing unless a new Savings Plan is purchased.
**Renewal date:** 2027-02-20
**Annual value:** $3,600 ($300/mo committed spend)

## Scope
Discounted committed compute for the firm's infrastructure (EC2 + Fargate).

## Renewal
Does not auto-renew. To keep the discount past 2027-02-20, purchase a new
Savings Plan before the commitment expires, otherwise compute reverts to
on-demand rates.
