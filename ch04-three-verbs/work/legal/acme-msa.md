# Master Services Agreement — Acme Corp

**Counterparty:** Acme Corporation
**Type:** MSA (Master Services Agreement)
**Effective date:** 2024-11-15
**Term:** 12 months, auto-renewing
**Auto-renew:** Yes — renews automatically each year on the anniversary
unless either party gives written notice at least 30 days before the
renewal date.
**Next renewal date:** 2026-11-15
**Annual value:** $84,000

landing-page production.

Renewal carries a 4.2% pricing increase per Section 6.2 (annual CPI
adjustment). To renegotiate or terminate, written notice must be
delivered no later than 30 days before the renewal date.
